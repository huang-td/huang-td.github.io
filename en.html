About Me

Qibo Xu received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Hunan University in 2025. He is currently a Lecturer at the School of Computer Science, Changsha University of Science and Technology.

His research focuses on the application of artificial intelligence and deep learning in intelligent photonics, aiming to build efficient and interpretable data-driven models for intelligent modeling and optimization of complex optical systems. He has published more than ten SCI-indexed papers in leading international journals including Journal of Lightwave Technology, Chaos, Solitons & Fractals, Physical Review A, and Optics Express.
